Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

Short version: Chisel Finance stores all your financial data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial information on any server. We have no account system and no analytics. Your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly export it yourself.

1. Who We Are

Chisel Finance is a Windows desktop application for personal debt tracking and payoff planning. It is developed and published as an independent software product. References to "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to the developer of Chisel Finance.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We collect absolutely nothing. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your debt balances, interest rates, or financial data
  • Your income, expenses, or budget information
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind
  • Payment or billing information (the app has no subscription backend)

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Chisel Finance — debts, balances, budgets, payment history, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage within the Electron application sandbox). This data:

  • Never leaves your device automatically
  • Is not synced to any cloud service by the application
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Remains entirely under your control at all times

You can export your data as a JSON backup file at any time via Settings → Export Backup, and delete all data via Settings → Clear All Data.

4. Optional Third-Party Services

Chisel Finance offers two optional integrations. Neither is required to use the app, and both are configured entirely with credentials you provide — we never hold or manage these credentials on any server.

Service Purpose Data sent Who controls it
Optional
Anthropic Claude API		 AI Advisor chat and personalized tips Your debt summary and budget totals are sent to Anthropic's API when you use AI features. No names or account numbers are included. You — using your own API key. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
Optional
Plaid Banking		 Automatic balance sync from your bank accounts Account balance data is fetched directly from Plaid using your own credentials. No transaction history is stored by the app. You — using your own Plaid developer credentials. Governed by Plaid's Privacy Policy.

If you do not configure either integration, no data is ever sent outside your device.

5. API Keys and Credentials

If you choose to use the optional AI or banking features, you provide your own API keys. These keys are stored in your device's local application data directory (the Electron userData path) using the operating system's standard file system. They are:

  • Never transmitted to the developer
  • Never stored on any external server
  • Only used to make direct calls from your device to the respective third-party API

6. Microsoft Store

When you acquire Chisel Finance through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect information related to your purchase and device as part of their platform. This is governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement and is separate from Chisel Finance itself.

7. Children's Privacy

Chisel Finance is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. As stated throughout this policy, we do not collect any personal information from anyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and update the policy within the app. Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how Chisel Finance handles your data, please reach out via the Microsoft Store support contact on the app's Store listing page.
